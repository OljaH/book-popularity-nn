The Hindi novella, "Suraj Ka Satwan Ghoda" by Dharamvir Bharti is highly acclaimed in the Hindi literary circles, for its highly experimental form of storytelling and well-etched out characters.
The stories are told by Manik Mulla, a supervisor at the Railway Mail Service who narrates the stories of three women he meets at three different points in life over a week in the afternoons.
The three women belong to different strata.
Suraj Ka Satwan Ghoda is a leisurely read which harks back to an age when the common modes of passing time were gossiping or listening to stories was the best activity.
Book is covering various aspects of life like love, relation etc. The way characters are connected to each other are very unique. There are number of plays done on this book so one can guess why this is so famous
