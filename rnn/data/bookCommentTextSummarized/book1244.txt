I read this book of poetry many, many years ago, having already read "Los heraldos negros," and I liked it then, so I thought to re-read it now, to see if my perspective has changed as much as I think it has.
"Trilce" is short for "Tristeza dulce," and it is the only book of poems that Vallejo had published in 1922, during his life time (the rest of his poetry was published as "Poesías completas" after his death in 1938).
This collection did not disappoint, and having read it years later was not a waste of time or effort, in that the changes in my own perceptions over the years proved to be self-revelatory.
