Attention, goodreads denizens: Ilf and Petrov's American Road Trip is NOT a different edition of Одноэтажная Америка/One-Storied America/Little Golden America.
It's an entirely different book.While some of the material overlaps between the two books, since they are based on the same experience, American Road Trip is much shorter--it is really more of a photo essay.
Unlike the in-print English edition of Little Golden America, American Road Trip is illustrated with Ilf's photos.Honestly, I felt like American Road Trip lost many of the best stories and portions of Ilf and Petrov's travelogue, and the added photographs didn't do much for me.
The introduction gave some interesting background, though.
