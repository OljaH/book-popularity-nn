His model of focusing on self-image, relationships, goals, attitude, work and desire (and how to improve each) allow you to focus on what is most important.
His chapters on goal-setting are some of the best I've read on the topic.
For the first time in a long time I actually believe I can achieve success.
Either way, it's a definite must-read.
