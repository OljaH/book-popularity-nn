Here again we receive and exhaustive account that covers the settlements, disputes, politics and lives of the new colonies and their inhabitants.
Similar to the previous one, this volume may fall victim to the confusion that comes with attempting to explain in detail such numerous government transfers and the politics behind them along the vast cast of characters involved in creating the colonial life.
Perhaps hard to follow at times, this volume, well written, doubtless still stands as a trustworthy continuation of the progression of the colonial expansion taking over the American land.
