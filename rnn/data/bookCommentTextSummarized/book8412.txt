This book is one of a box set about the naughty little sister.
The books are entertaining and enjoyable with enough mischief to truly rope in all children.
The setting of the books and the surroundings are well described and charming, a stark contrast to the naughtiness of the title character.
This book is embedded in the innocence of growing up , a contrast to so many books where the protagonists are superheroes or live in exceptional circumstances.
In these books the characters do simple things in normal, relatable environments, which makes them perfect for comprehension and discussion exercises with all children in a class.
