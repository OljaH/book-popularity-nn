Van Gogh sees beauty in the workaday world and "regular people," far moreso than in models and more typically-expressed concepts of art.Stone's story takes Van Gogh through various stages of his life as he develops his art, gets to know his fellow artists (Toulouse-Lautrec, Gauguin (with whom he rather infamously lived for a while in Arles), and discovers his various passions.
