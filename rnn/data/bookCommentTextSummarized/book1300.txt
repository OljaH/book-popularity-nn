Between the Polk-Lillard book (Montessori from the Start) and this one, I think there is a stark contrast in the approach to the child. I feel Montessori is far more respectful of individual variation and developing the relationships between adults and children. I felt Polk- Lillard was so scientific in terms of her needs of the child that it left parents and children at an arms length. Polk- Lillard had more specific activity and toy recommendations, Montessori had better development and relationship information!
