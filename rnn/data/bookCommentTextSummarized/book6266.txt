Don Camillo: Mondo piccolo = The Little World of Don Camillo (Don Camillo #1), Giovannino GuareschiDon Camillo is a character created by the Italian writer and journalist Giovannino Guareschi, whose name, and some of his character, is based on an actual Roman Catholic priest, World War II partisan and detainee at the concentration camps of Dachau and Mauthausen, named Don Camillo Valota (1912–1998).
Guareschi was also inspired by Don Alessandro Parenti, a priest of Trepalle, near the Swiss border.
