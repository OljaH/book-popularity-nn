I have read this book many times and never tire of it. My favourite one since my childhood.
Dashenka, the wire-haired fox terrier enchants and then terrifies his mother and humans within days of her arrival. Capek, better known for his futuristic novel, R.U.R., source of the word "robot", chronicles the early months of his bounding puppy in words, photographs and drawings. The tale is whimsical, delightful in its simplicity, somewhat sentimental and a lovely distraction from more serious pursuits. Children love having Dashenka read to them.
