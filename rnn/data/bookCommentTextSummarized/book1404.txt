The novel itself says the "Amok" is Malay in origin and indeed it is also considered a Tagalog word.
Another Tagalog word for it is "hurumentado"--someone doing a suicidal run that involves indiscriminate attacks/killings and usually ends up in the hurumentado's own death.
Zweig's Amok, a doctor of medicine, theorized that the cause of this "going on the rampage, a sort of human form of rabies...a paroxysm of murderous, mindless monomania which isn't comparable to any alcoholic poisoning" has something to do "with the climate, with that sultry, oppressive atmosphere that plays on your nerves like a thunderstorm until they snap..."It took more than the climate for the doctor to run Amok here, however.
