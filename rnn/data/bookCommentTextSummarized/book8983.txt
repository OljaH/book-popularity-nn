I think I read this when I was in primary school, 4th grade to be exact.
Of course I think I was one of the three people who actually read it but at the time I was low on books and I have to admit that the legends of Gods and Goddesses always fascinated me.
The book totally did a number on my young and impressionable mind, and I think I managed to read this whole thing in about a week, which you have to admit, for a 9 year old girl it's pretty quick.
Loved it. Knew it by heart. I still do, after 8 years. Forever taught me the Gods of Ancient Greek and the heroes.
