However, what was most interesting to me was to read this after having read in Sebastian's journal the process of writing this book and the blocks he encountered.
Often, in the novel, an idea that was just a two-liner in his journal about one of his real-life friends, gets expanded into a page of reflections by one of the characters.
Paul's first skiing experience is just a more lyrical version of Sebastian's own noted in the journal.
Even the places named as part of Paul's past (Balcic, Predeal, the timing and towns along his trip to Belgium) match the places that Sebastian had visited earlier, as read in previous years in the Journal.
