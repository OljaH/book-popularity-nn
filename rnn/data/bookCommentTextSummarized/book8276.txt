This is literally a rant/burn book that Bulgakov churned out in a few hours.
You get the point (that the theater scene in Moscow is ridiculous) in about twenty pages and unless you know quite a bit about twentieth century Soviet theater it will be pretty difficult to pick up on his many detailed burns on specific people.
I'd say skip this one unless you are a dedicated fan or you wrote a dissertation on Moscow theater in the first half of the twentieth century.
