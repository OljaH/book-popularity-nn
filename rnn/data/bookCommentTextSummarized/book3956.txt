I think this would be a really good book for someone who isn't familiar with Nabokov, or at least predisposed to like his oeuvre.

He builds up the tension in a way that evokes Lolita or Despair, and of course it never goes in the direction you think it might.
Nabokov toys with us, but those who submit will be rewarded.
