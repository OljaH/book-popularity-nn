I would definitely recommend The Locomotive written by Julian Tuwim. This is one of the picture books which I keep in memory. I remember that as a child I really enjoyed the pictures, since they were very colorful, eye-catching, and easily expressed the meaning of the text.I was also amazed by the rhyme, the pace, the spirit of the story. The relationship between images and words in Tuwim's picture book is clearly visible even for a very young reader, therefore, it can be read both by children and by adults.
