Sadly, despite the profundity of Budekin's message, Swastika Night doesn't hold up aesthetically.

Swastika Night is a dialogue driven and philosophically laden tale of a pilgrimage of an Englishman named Alfred to Germany, the center of the Holy Nazi Empire.
During his pilgrimage, Alfred encounters a Nazi knight who has doubts about the effects of holding women to an animal-like status.
