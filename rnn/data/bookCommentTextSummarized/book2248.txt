Pagnol wrote the conclusion to Marius and Fanny’s story after meeting with an elderly lady who worked as a prop supplier on his movie set: she told him she did not want to die before she knew how the story really ended, so he hurried up and finished the story.When the curtain falls on “Fanny”, Marius leaves, realizing what a terrible mistake he has made and Fanny remains in her passion-free but affectionate marriage with Panisse for the sake of her son.
It opens twenty years after the events of “Fanny”: Panisse is dying, and on his deathbed, makes Fanny promise that she will tell “their” son Césariot who his real father is.
