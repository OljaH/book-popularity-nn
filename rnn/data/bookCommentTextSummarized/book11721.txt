When it comes to humor and wit, no one can live up to the standard set by Ibn e Insha.
Urdu ki Aakhri Kitab (the last book of urdu) comprises of stories, poems and proses and covers area from nature, geography, history, science, mathematics etc.
These stories, poems and proses display the unparalleled wisdom and wit of Ibn e Insha shrouded in a healthy criticism.
The stories of Alexender and Khizer, Mahmud Ghaznavi and Firdosi, and union is strength are the best part of my experience reading this timelessly classic book.
