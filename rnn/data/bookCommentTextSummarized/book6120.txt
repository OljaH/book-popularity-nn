Signs and Symbols is a short story about a couple whose son has attempted suicide.
The story is full of symbolism and the author leaves an open ending that makes the reader try to find the missing piece of the puzzle.
The conflict is not just between the parents and their boy, but also between them and the world.
They all live in reality, but at the same time they are separated mentally or physically.
Their boy is a symbol of a severe illness, that makes him believe that every object in this world is conspiring against him.
Which makes us feel, in a way, the emotional conflict of any jewish man who suffered from the Nazi army.
considering that his parents where jewish, who struggle through the World War II.
So, as his parents faces reality, he faces delusions that keeps him out of this world.

From the title of this short story we can understand that it has to do with signs and symbols.
However, the question that seems to be posed by Nabokov is: aren't we all threatened by symbols?
Aunt Rosa, a victim of rates of criminality (media propaganda), the belief that beautiful weeds have life of their own and cannot hide from a farmer, the mother who confuses the written symbols: numbers and letters.
In the end, aren't we all trapped in a world of symbols we made?This is an interesting short story, dealing with a crucial subject but rather suggesting a question than giving an answer.
