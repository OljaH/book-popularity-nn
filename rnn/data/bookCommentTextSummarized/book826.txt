You'll have to disregard the dated terms like "savage" for peoples not of Western (white) lineage. Beyond that, all the accounts are from white missionaries as far as I can tell, so it's all blurry and smeared from their interpretations. I felt like the attempts at a conclusion were useless and assumptions were made as fact. As interesting as it is to hear other cultures' mythos, this isn't going to be a series I continue to read, sadly.Also? Muskrat for the win.
