A light hearted, good read!!
A collection of three distinct stories dealing with three different ideas but the unifying factor is Basheer's language which is taken from our lives itself.
The first story speaks of a person who becomes a celebrity due to his extremely long nose and is clearly a satire on the hypocritical stand of media and people on such issues.
It is a fun-filled journey where we witness many people taking advantage of him and the people being manipulated by the media.
What is great about the book is the way Basheer uses a few page(32) to bring emotions in the reader's mind with minimum words.
Three nice short stories in typical Basheer style, simple , funny , thought provoking and to the point. Basheer is so good at saying all he wanted to say with the least amount of words, which makes reading his strories twice as enjoyable.
