The first half of the story is set in Persia (present day Iran) and Central Asia in the 11th century, and revolves around the scientist, philosopher, and poet Omar Khayyám.
It recounts the creation of his Rubaiyat throughout the history of the Seljuk Empire, his interactions with historical figures such as Vizir Nizam al-Mulk and Hassan al-Sabbah of the order of the Assassins, and his love affair with a female poet of the Samarkand court.
The second half of the story documents the efforts of a fictional American named Benjamin O.

Khayyam was a philosopher, poet, consultant to leaders, astrologer, scientist of his day.

In the second half, we are transported to more modern times, to a Persia in a state of unrest, and to a search for the Rubaiyaat which has not been seen for centuries.
