This is the book that changed my life. I'd been an extremely neurotic person most of my life and this book described me to a T. I was in Olin Library on the Cornell campus when I sat down to read this book and couldn't put it down. I finally had a way out of my jail b/c there were people who understood the kinds of feelings and experience I was having. A great weight was lifted of my shoulders, but it took years of very hard work to make progress and the work continues to this day.
Pretty much everyone you know, including yourself, is described in detail here, sadly. Should be taught in grade schools.
